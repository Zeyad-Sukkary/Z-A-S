About Z-A-S

Z-A-S is the personal project of Zeyad Ahmed El-Sukkary, a passionate 15-year-old web developer and aspiring software engineer. Built from scratch with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this site showcases Zeyad’s growing skills in responsive design, UI/UX, and dynamic content filtering. Beyond coding, Zeyad is driven by a dream to study at Stanford and one day Start his own Company. This website is a testament to his ambition and love for technology.
